Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 T Wired Brain Coffee Hot Cocoa
  • 8 oz Milk
  • Marshmallows

Steps

  1. Scoop Hot Cocoa Mix into a mug.
  2. Zohrej mlieko na ohni.
  3. Zohrievaj do zovretia.
  4. Pridaj marshmallows ak nutne nevyhnutne je.
